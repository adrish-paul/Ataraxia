# ATARAXIA — BRAND IDENTITY DOCUMENT & BIBLE

> This document defines what Ataraxia is. It should never change. Every future prompt, feature, and design decision must align with this identity.

---

## Project Information

*   **Project Name:** Ataraxia
*   **Design System Codename:** **Lunaflora** (moon + flowers) 🌙🌸
*   **Meaning:** The name *Ataraxia* originates from Ancient Greek philosophy and refers to a state of serene calmness, emotional balance, and freedom from mental disturbance. The application must embody this philosophy in every aspect of its design and functionality.
*   **Tagline:** *Find peace, one quiet moment at a time.*
*   **Alternative Taglines (for marketing):**
    *   Your sanctuary in a noisy world.
    *   Breathe. Reflect. Simply be.
    *   A gentle place for your mind.
    *   Pause. Breathe. Bloom.
    *   Calm begins here.

---

## Mission & Vision

### Mission Statement
Ataraxia exists to provide a peaceful digital sanctuary where users can slow down, reconnect with themselves, and cultivate emotional wellbeing through mindful experiences. 

Rather than encouraging productivity or achievement, Ataraxia encourages presence, reflection, and self-kindness. The application should never make users feel pressured, guilty, or overwhelmed.

### Vision Statement
To become one of the most beautifully crafted wellness applications on Android by combining elegant design, emotionally supportive experiences, and modern technology into one cohesive sanctuary.

## The Lunaflora Design System (Visual Identity)

The UI language communicates elegance through restraint.

### Visual Aesthetic
*   Soft, Premium, Minimal, Dreamlike, Organic, Calming, Breathable, and Beautiful.

### Color Palette (High-Level)
*   **Primary:** Dusty Lavender, Moonlight Indigo, Soft Lilac
*   **Secondary:** Warm Ivory, Pearl White, Misty Sage, Blush Pink, Pale Peach, Powder Blue
*   **Dark Mode:** Deep Indigo, Muted Purple, Warm Gray *(Avoid pure black)*

### Icons & Illustrations
*   **Icon Style:** Rounded, Minimal, Elegant, Outlined, Soft. Avoid aggressive iconography.
*   **Illustration Style:** Nature-inspired (petals, moon, stars, clouds, flowers, butterflies, lanterns, trees, gentle waves, soft hills). No cartoon mascots or exaggerated expressions. Illustrations support the atmosphere rather than dominate.

### Animation & Sound
*   **Animation Philosophy:** Animations must feel like breathing. Slow movement, fading, and gliding. No popping, jumping, or excessive spinning.
*   **Sound Philosophy:** Button sounds must be soft and almost inaudible. Ambient sounds should be natural, continuous, loop seamlessly, and contain no sudden volume changes.

---

## Interaction & Writing Style

### Interaction Philosophy
The application never rushes the user. Animations move slowly, buttons feel soft, navigation is predictable, writing is uninterrupted, and focus sessions remove distractions. Every interaction communicates patience.

### Writing Style
All text inside Ataraxia should sound like it was written by a kind friend.

| Avoid | Replace With |
| :--- | :--- |
| Complete | Finished peacefully |
| Done | Saved safely |
| Failed | Let's try again |
| Expired / Missed | Whenever you're ready |
| Error | Take your time / Welcome back |

### Brand Voice
*   Warm, Brief, Calm. Never lecture, never motivate aggressively, never pressure.
*   *Instead of:* "You need to meditate."
*   *Say:* "A quiet moment might help."

## Rules and Promises

### Things Ataraxia Never Does:
1.  Never creates fear of missing out (FOMO).
2.  Never celebrates long streaks.
3.  Never compares users.
4.  Never ranks users.
5.  Never pressures consistency.
6.  Never displays advertisements.
7.  Never interrupts meditation.
8.  Never forces registration.
9.  Never uses loud notifications.
10. Never uses harsh colors.
11. Never overwhelms users.

### The Ataraxia Promise:
Every feature added to Ataraxia must answer this question:
**"Will this make the user feel calmer?"**
*If the answer is no, the feature should be redesigned or removed.*

### Development Principles:
*   Quality over quantity.
*   Consistency over novelty.
*   Emotion over efficiency.
*   Calm over complexity.
*   Accessibility over decoration.
*   Privacy over data collection.
